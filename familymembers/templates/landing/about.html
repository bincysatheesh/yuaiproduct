Zambia Malayalee Cultural Association

Est. 1980

Founded in 1980 and registered under the Societies Act of Zambia, the Zambia Malayalee Cultural Association (ZMCA) has been a cornerstone of cultural preservation and philanthropy. Our mission is twofold: to celebrate the vibrant heritage of Kerala—'God's Own Country'—and to make a tangible impact through charitable initiatives in Zambia.

We bridge continents through cultural exchange, offering members a platform to engage in traditions, arts, and community-building activities. From Onam festivals to skill-development workshops, ZMCA fosters connections that transcend borders.

Beyond cultural events, we actively contribute to Zambian society through education sponsorships, healthcare initiatives, and disaster relief efforts. Our members are united by a shared commitment to service and cultural pride.

Today, ZMCA stands as a testament to the enduring bond between Kerala and Zambia—a living bridge of shared values, mutual respect, and collective growth.
