Zambia Malayalee Cultural Association (ZMCA)

Established in 1980 and registered under the Societies Act of Zambia, the Zambia Malayalee Cultural Association (ZMCA) stands as a testament to the enduring commitment to cultural exchange and charitable endeavors.

Rooted in the vibrant heritage of "God's Own Country", Kerala, the association actively engages in diverse charitable and cultural activities to foster a strong connection with Zambia. With a core focus on preserving and upholding the rich identity and traditions of Kerala, ZMCA encourages its members to participate wholeheartedly.
